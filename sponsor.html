The 2002 National Student Association of Computing Machinery Computing Job Fair will be held this year from 9:00AM - 5:00PM on Friday, October 18, 2002 at the University of Illinois in Urbana-Champaign in the Digital Computing Lab Atrium. This job fair, held annually for the past eight years, is the premier job fair for finding computer science and computer engineering students for internships and full-time/part-time employment. Not only will participation guarantee you access to the best and brightest students at the University of Illinois, but it also will provide access to other students from schools all around the nation who arrive for the weekend to attend the job fair and the associated conference. Last year alone, attendance at this job fair was estimated at 1,000 students, of which a vast majority were students in Computer Science, MIS, and Computer Engineering disciplines.

There are two donation levels job fair offers to corporate participants. A $500 level for normal participation in job fair is offered. At this level, the company is given a booth, may bring up to five recruiters, and will also recieve a copy of our resume book. For companies wishing to develop a more long term relationship with ACM@UIUC, we offer a full sponsorship level at $1000 which guarantees priority placement at the job fair, the use of private interview rooms, and the placement of your company’s logo and name on all job fair publicity.

Please direct all queries regarding the job fair to Vilas Dhar, at acm@uiuc.edu.
