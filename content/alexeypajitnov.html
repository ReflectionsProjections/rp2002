Alexey Pajitnov

Alexey Pajitnov, a computer engineer from Russia, was key to the development the popular game Tetris. Pajitnov developed Tetris while he worked at the Moscow Academy of Sciences in 1985. Pajitnov moved to USA in 1991 and founded the Tetris Company with Henk Rogers. He helped design the puzzles in the Super NES versions of Yoshi's Cookie and designed the game Pandora's Box, which incorporates more traditional jigsaw-style puzzles. Pajitnov has been credited with the roles Design, Support and Administration because of his vast experience with gaming software design. Pajitnov personal preference is puzzle games and he is currently working on a second version of Pandora's Box. He is currently working for Microsoft's gaming division
