Boris Shakhnovich

Boris Shakhnovich was born in Puschino, Russia, in 1979 and came to the United States in 1990 with his family. He received his Bachelor's degree in Computational Biophysics from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. He is currently a student of Bioinformatics at Boston University, working towards his Ph.D. His interests include evolutionary molecular biology and intellectual property rights for computationally intensive fields.
